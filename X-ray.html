    X-ray Phase Imaging

    —Momose Laboratory(IMARAM, Tohoku University), under the instruction of Prof. Atsushi Momose

1. Introduction

X-ray Phase Imaging is a new measurement method using synchrotron radiation X-rays, which are known for its useful features; brightness and high directionality. We use two gratings and a sensor to detect the phase of the defraction from the object. Like computed tomography, by illumination every direction of the object, we can got the 3-dimentional structure of this object with its internal information(See Fig.1).
